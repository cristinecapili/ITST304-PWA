Web Based Barangay Inofrmation Management system for barangay kanluran calauan Laguna

The development of Barangay Information and e-services management system for barangay kanluran is important because it can help barangay officials make their services more accessible to the public online, which is beneficial not only to the community but also creates well-founded productivity. Barangay offices must be automated so that they can efficiently deliver services to the local community.
